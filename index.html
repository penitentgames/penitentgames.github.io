Locked out of the system and facing eminent assimilation, you must hack to survive. Unlock hidden abilities, build your forces, fight the AI overlords... or each other, who cares? You have free will... for now.

In this Groundhog Day from Hell, you play as the last of an order of holy warriors sworn to protect the once great kingdom of Prav. Slay demons and uncover hidden abilities as you scour the lands of Asha to recover the four Earth Stones. These sacred stones hold the key to reuniting the world under the source and creator of all, the good lord Ahba. Act fast as the dark lord Daal and his forces grow stronger by the minute. Offer prayers at shrines to receive blessings and find memory stones to remember them when you die.
