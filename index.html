A THPS Roguelike where your board transforms into a gun.

Assemble your team of unlikely heroes in this coop action shooter set in a cyberpunk universe. Armed with only your skateboard, fight through a sequence ef levels on your way to defeat the technocratic overlord, Apollo.

All skateboards do tricks, but yours has something special under its griptape. Charge you board with sick moves, then transform it into a gun and lay waste to robot scum.

The clock is ticking, and Apollo wants you dead. Collect random upgrades to help you survive the ever increasing barrage of destruction headed your way. Find the access key, make your way to the next portal, fight the boss, rinse and repeat.
