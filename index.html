A skateboarding game where you score big to add time to the clock. Combine random items to boost your score in unexpected ways.

Inspired by the THPS series and Risk of Rain 2, Cybordz is a fast-paced skateboarding game focused on reaching increasingly insane scores. Random items help you earn more points so you can keep the clock going. Can you make it to the end before time runs out?

About Penitent Games

A humble company of one, Penitent Games aims to make challenging games that can be enjoyed by the whole family. Games have always been a way for me to bond with friends and family, and I hope to bring people closer together with laughter and surprises.

I’m a father of two with a third on the way! I work on Cybordz in my spare time, so progress may be slower than you’d like. Rest assured, I’m committed to making Cybordz an awesome game for an awesome fanbase.
